Our Simple 4 Step Process

Select Your Style

We provide a range of styles that are suitable to enhance any housing or commercial space. Our range of custom blinds includes cedar blinds, sunscreen, roller/blockout, shutters, fauxwood, paulownia, motorised and vertical blinds.

Measure & Quote

We provide free measure and quote within the Auckland region with no obligation. Contact us to arrange an in-home consultation. However, if you live outside of Auckland and are happy to measure and install the blinds yourself refer to our how to measure section for details on how to measure your windows for made to measure blinds.

Order Confirmation

For an order confirmation, we will contact you via email or phone with a quote. If youre happy, we will then process the order with a required deposit and the remaining balance is due at the time of installation.

Installation

All blinds are manufactured within 10 working days from the date that your order is confirmed, with your deposit received. Alternatively, if you need blinds urgently we are flexible. Once your blinds are ready we will contact you to arrange installation or feel free to pick your blinds up. We ship nationwide.
